Hard rules

Screens only where the camera faces them. A sweepstakes TV appears only when the shot looks at a wall that actually holds one. Never float one in to fill space.

No partition. There is no dividing wall or pass-through.

No CCTV monitor. It exists in the photos; never render it.

Render the future state. The "THE FLAME" mural behind the bar is being removed, so that wall is plain. Storefront glass stays clean, no placards.

House light: saturated magenta/violet on walls and ceiling, cyan-blue spill from the machine LED trim, warm amber accents, deep controlled blacks.
